# resume-services

Resume Righting helps job seekers rewrite their professional story for better interviews and faster offers.

Resume Services is a small professional services project focused on rewriting resumes to improve narrative clarity, market positioning, and interview outcomes. The initial scope is to build templates, workflows, and content components that support repeatable fulfillment rather than one-off freelancing.

## What This Project Includes

* Core resume rewrite framework
* Template library for resumes and LinkedIn profiles
* Narrative extraction worksheets
* Targeting and job alignment tools
* ATS and formatting considerations
* Output delivery process

## Target Users

* Professionals who are interviewing or preparing to interview
* Individuals switching industries or roles
* Candidates underselling their experience due to weak framing
* Referrals who need fast turnaround and clean positioning

## Project Goals

* Standardize intake and rewrite workflow
* Improve client outcomes through better narrative framing
* Reduce rewrite time through reusable components
* Experiment with light automation for certain steps
