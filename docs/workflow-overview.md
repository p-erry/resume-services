# Workflow Overview

Workflow designed for asynchronous fulfillment, clean client intake, and predictable output.

## Phase 1: Intake

Client provides:

* Existing resume (PDF or DOCX)
* Target role(s) or job descriptions
* Optional: LinkedIn profile URL
* Optional: role pivot context

Delivered via:

* Form upload (preferred)
* Email handoff (fallback)

## Phase 2: Narrative Extraction

Goal is to gather missing signal:

* Outcomes and achievements
* Cross-functional leadership
* Scale (dollars, teams, tech)
* Target leveling and seniority

Tools:

* Narrative questionnaire
* Role targeting worksheet

## Phase 3: Rewrite & Rebuild

Outputs produced:

* Resume rewrite in DOCX
* Resume PDF for submission
* Keyword alignment for ATS
* Optional LinkedIn sections

Focus areas:

* Clarity
* Positioning
* Scope
* Impact
* Transferability

## Phase 4: Review & Revision

Client receives the first draft and provides edits or corrections.

Bounded revision cycles maintain clarity and turnaround.

Revisions handled async via:

* Commenting
* Highlighting
* Change requests

## Phase 5: Delivery

Final delivery includes:

* DOCX (master copy)
* PDF (submission copy)
* Optional artifacts from tier selection

Optional follow-on:

* Interview prep
* Role targeting analysis
* LinkedIn tuning
