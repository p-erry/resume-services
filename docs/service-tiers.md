# Service Tiers

Initial tier design for Resume Righting focused on side-business fulfillment, async workflow, and clean client expectations.

## Entry: Resume Refresh

For professionals with an existing resume needing modernization and polish.

**Includes**

* Resume rewrite based on current material
* Formatting and structure improvements
* ATS considerations and keyword refinement
* PDF and DOCX delivery

**Turnaround**
3–4 days

**Revisions**
1 cycle (24–48 hour SLA)

---

## Core: Resume Rebuild

For professionals underselling their experience or targeting new roles.

**Includes Everything in Refresh, Plus**

* Narrative extraction from brief intake questionnaire
* Job targeting adjustments
* Bullet restructuring for outcomes vs responsibilities
* LinkedIn summary rewrite (optional add-on)

**Turnaround**
5–7 days

**Revisions**
2 cycles (48 hour SLA)

---

## Premium: Targeted Interview Package

For professionals actively interviewing and needing alignment across market touchpoints.

**Includes Everything in Rebuild, Plus**

* Cover letter template for target role(s)
* LinkedIn headline and about rewrite
* Talking points for interview framing
* Optional: Role-to-resume alignment matrix

**Turnaround**
7–10 days

**Revisions**
2 cycles + micro adjustments during interview window

---

## Add-Ons

* LinkedIn tuning only
* Cover letter only
* Role targeting analysis
* Speed upgrade (48 hour or 72 hour)
* Additional revision cycles

---

## Notes for Later Pricing Strategy

For side-business economics:

* Avoid hourly pricing
* Avoid unlimited revisions
* Anchor on turnaround + revisions + deliverables

Stripe or manual invoicing can be layered later.
